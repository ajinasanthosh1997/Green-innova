Sustainable Façade Solutions

Green Innova specializes in EIFS using Terraco solutions. Founded in 2013, we are an ISO-certified company (ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001), an NOCN-approved training center, and a member of the US Green Building Council.

Exterior Insulation Finishing Systems are energy-efficient thermal wrappings applied to building exteriors, finished with durable and decorative coatings. Terraco, a pioneer since the 1980s, provides systems with global certifications (EOTA, BBA, US FED) ensuring peak performance.

EIFS are valued in both hot and cold climates for their sustainability, return on investment, and design flexibility.
